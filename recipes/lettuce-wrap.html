Lettuce Wrap

Description

A basic and tasty recipe for a low-carb, high protein dinner your family will love!

Tastes great wrapped in lettuce, or on top of a bowl of plain or brown rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground turkey or beef
  • 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 green onions
  • 1 can sliced water chestnuts, drained and diced
  • salt, pepper, and ginger, to taste
  • freshly washed and dried lettuce leaves

Directions

  1. Preheat a wok to medium-high heat. Add ground meat and cook until browned, about 3-5 minutes.
  2. Stir in hoisin sauce, soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, garlic, and onion, cooking until onions are translucent. Add water chestnuts and green onions and stir, cooking until tender (about 1-2 minutes). Season with ginger, salt, and pepper to taste.
  3. Spoon mixture over lettuce leaves, or on top of rice, depending on preference.
