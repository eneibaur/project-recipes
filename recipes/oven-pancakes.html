Oven Pancakes

Description

A household staple - incredibly easy to make, modify, and a treat for everyone!

Recipe is written per person.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 pinch (1/4 tsp) salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Place butter in a small bread pan, placing the pan into the oven.
  2. Whisk the eggs, milk, flour, and salt into a consistent mixture.
  3. Remove pan from oven. Tilt the pan so the butter spreads into an even layer across the bottom of the pan. Pour the batter mixture into the butter.
  4. Bake batter for 20-25 minutes, until the top edges are golden brown. The pancake will puff up as it bakes.
  5. Remove quickly from the pan, dusting with powdered sugar. Cover with desired toppings, and enjoy!
